Project Title: Bharat Board Games – Reviving India's Forgotten Strategic Heritage

Build a complete, modern, responsive Progressive Web Application (PWA) that digitally preserves and revives ancient Indian board games through interactive gameplay, historical storytelling, and cultural education.

PROJECT VISION

Create a premium heritage-inspired gaming platform that showcases India's forgotten board games and their historical significance. The platform should blend authentic Indian culture, mythology, history, and strategic gameplay into a modern digital experience.

The overall experience should feel like entering an ancient royal Indian palace, temple corridor, or heritage museum where traditional games are preserved and brought to life through technology.
