# 🎲 Kridaभूमि (Kridabhoomi): The Ancient Arcade

### Reviving India's Forgotten Strategic Heritage Through Technology

A Progressive Web Application dedicated to preserving, digitizing, and celebrating ancient Indian board games through interactive gameplay, cultural storytelling, and historical education.

![Kridaभूमि Banner](./assets/banner.png)

## 📖 Problem Statement

Many ancient Indian board games that once shaped strategic thinking, social interaction, and cultural storytelling are slowly disappearing from public memory.

Kridaभूमि aims to digitally preserve and revive these forgotten treasures, making them accessible to modern audiences through an engaging and educational gaming experience.

By blending authentic gameplay mechanics with immersive storytelling, the platform bridges the gap between generations while celebrating India's rich gaming heritage.

## ✨ Features

- 🎮 Play authentic Indian board games
- 📜 Interactive historical storytelling
- 🏛 Learn the cultural significance of each game
- 📍 Explore the regional origins of traditional games
- 📱 Fully responsive Progressive Web App (PWA)
- 🌐 Installable on mobile and desktop
- 🎨 Heritage-inspired UI/UX
- ⚡ Fast and modern web experience
- 📚 Educational game-learning modules
- 🇮🇳 Preservation of Indian cultural heritage

## 🇮🇳 Cultural Impact

Kridaभूमि is more than a gaming platform.

It serves as a digital archive of India's rich gaming heritage, helping younger generations reconnect with traditional knowledge systems, strategic thinking, and cultural history.

By combining technology with heritage preservation, Kridaभूमि contributes to the protection and promotion of India's intangible cultural assets.
